About This Experience

Your mind never stops. Here, it finally gets to rest.

From the moment you wake up, your mind is already running — to-do lists, worries, replaying conversations, the endless scroll. By night you're exhausted, but your head still won't switch off.

Clear The Noise is a calm, guided 3-hour evening, away from your usual routine. Through simple awareness practices, gentle reflection, and breathing techniques, you'll learn how to step back from the mental chatter — instead of being pulled around by it.

It's not a lecture. It's not a strict meditation class. It's a warm, safe space where you actually do the work — in real time, with others who feel exactly the same way.

You'll walk out lighter, calmer, and clearer — more like yourself than you've felt in a long time.
